## Drama

### 1. **Succession** (2018-2023)
- **IMDB:** 8.7/10
- **Rotten Tomatoes:** 95% Average | Season 4: 97%
- **Overview:** The Roy family battles for control of their media empire as their aging father begins to step back from the company.
- **Reviews:**
  - **Critical:** "A masterclass in writing, acting, and character development. One of the defining dramas of the decade."

- **Awards:** Won multiple Emmy Awards including Outstanding Drama Series.
